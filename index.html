Road Racer

Build & Race

Build your track, then race!

How to Play

Building Your Track

Drag track pieces from the library onto the grid. Press R to rotate pieces. Connect them to form a closed loop with a start/finish line.

Racing Controls

Arrow Keys / WASD - Accelerate, Brake/Reverse, Steer
SPACE + Steer - Drift

Goal

Complete 3 laps before the AI racers to win!
